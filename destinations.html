Top Destinations

Explore popular tourist destinations across South India

Kerala

God's Own Country

Experience the serene backwaters, lush hill stations, and pristine beaches of Kerala. Our car rental services cover popular destinations including:

Munnar Alleppey Kochi Thekkady Wayanad Kovalam Kumarakom Varkala

Travel Time from Coimbatore: 10-12 hours | Best Season: October to March

Tamil Nadu

Land of Temples

Discover ancient temples, hill stations, and coastal beauty of Tamil Nadu. Affordable car hire for exploring the rich cultural heritage:

Ooty Kodaikanal Madurai Rameswaram Kanyakumari Yercaud Coimbatore Thanjavur

Popular Routes: Coimbatore-Ooty, Coimbatore-Rameswaram | Best Season: Year-round

Karnataka

Heritage & Nature

From Bangalore's tech hub to Mysore's royal palaces and Coorg's coffee plantations. Reliable cab services connecting major Karnataka attractions:

Bangalore Mysore Coorg Hampi Chikmagalur Gokarna Mangalore Badami

Travel Time from Coimbatore: 5-6 hours to Bangalore | Best Season: October to February

Andhra Pradesh

Spiritual & Scenic

Visit famous temples, scenic valleys, and coastal attractions of Andhra Pradesh. Professional drivers familiar with all major pilgrimage routes:

Tirupati Vijayawada Visakhapatnam Araku Valley Srisailam Amaravati Anantapur Lepakshi

Travel Time from Coimbatore: 2-3 hours to Tirupati | Best Season: September to March
