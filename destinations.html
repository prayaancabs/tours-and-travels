Top Destinations

Discover the most sought-after travel destinations across South India with Prayaan Cabs — your trusted partner for safe, scenic, and seamless journeys.

Kerala

God's Own Country

Kerala is where nature whispers through emerald backwaters, misty hill stations, and golden beaches. From romantic escapes to family holidays, explore Kerala’s serenity with our comfortable cab services covering every major destination.

Munnar Alleppey Kochi Thekkady Wayanad Kovalam Kumarakom Varkala

Best Season: October – March

Tamil Nadu

Land of Temples & Hills

Rich in culture and spirituality, Tamil Nadu welcomes travelers with its majestic temples, tranquil hill stations, and coastal wonders. With Prayaan Cabs, explore iconic heritage routes and scenic drives from Coimbatore to every corner of the state.

Ooty Kodaikanal Madurai Rameswaram Kanyakumari Yercaud Coimbatore Thanjavur

Popular Routes: Coimbatore–Ooty | Coimbatore–Rameswaram
Best Season: All year round

Karnataka

Heritage Meets Nature

Whether it’s the charm of Mysore’s palaces, the calm of Coorg’s coffee plantations, or the modern vibe of Bangalore — Karnataka offers something for everyone. Our cab services ensure a comfortable ride through scenic routes and heritage trails.

Bangalore Mysore Coorg Hampi Chikmagalur Gokarna Mangalore Badami

Best Season: October – February

Andhra Pradesh

Sacred & Scenic Wonders

Home to some of India’s most revered temples and coastal getaways, Andhra Pradesh combines spirituality and natural charm. From the divine aura of Tirupati to the serene Araku Valley, Prayaan Cabs offers safe and punctual transfers for all travel types.

Tirupati Vijayawada Visakhapatnam Araku Valley Srisailam Amaravati Anantapur Lepakshi

Best Season: September – March
